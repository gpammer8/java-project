About Us

PhotoSynth Telehealth was founded in 2022 by Garrett Pammer and is based in Denver, CO. In the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic, the importance of Telehealth and virtual communications became widely apparent. PhotoSynth Telehealth operates within the medical community to stay involved with new concerns and how to best manage patient and medical professional relationships.
